Payment & Pricing

Payment methods, pricing, currency, and billing information

We ship to most countries worldwide where tobacco import is legal. However, shipping restrictions may apply to certain regions due to local regulations. Please contact us to confirm if we can ship to your specific location.

Delivery times vary by destination. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days and delivery takes 5-15 business days depending on your location and selected shipping method. Express shipping options are available for faster delivery.

Yes, all orders include tracking information. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can track your package through our website or the carrier's tracking system.

We carry a wide selection of international tobacco brands including premium cigarettes, cigars, and tobacco products from major manufacturers. Our catalog includes both popular and specialty brands from various regions.

All our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors. We maintain proper storage conditions to ensure freshness and quality. Products are stored in climate-controlled facilities.

Yes, we can often source specific products upon request. Please contact our customer service team with details about the products you're looking for, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways.

Our listed prices do not include local taxes, duties, or customs fees that may apply in your country. These additional charges are the responsibility of the customer and will be collected by customs authorities upon delivery.

All prices are displayed in USD (US Dollars). However, your bank or payment provider may convert the amount to your local currency at their current exchange rate when processing the payment.

You must be at least 21 years old (or the legal smoking age in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher) to purchase tobacco products. Age verification is required for all orders and will be checked upon delivery.

We work with experienced logistics partners to ensure compliance with international shipping regulations. All necessary customs documentation is provided, but customers are responsible for understanding and complying with their local import laws.

In the rare event that an order is seized due to local regulations, we will work with you to resolve the issue. Depending on the circumstances, we may offer to reship the order or provide store credit for future purchases.
